Saint Mary's University Hack-A-Week 2014

"The Hack-a-week event at Saint Mary's University offers valuable hands-on experience for students, and shows how our educational institutions can work with industry to create real life experiences and opportunities. It is of true benefit to all involved." - Kelly Regan, MLA

In 2013, Saint Mary's changed the format of its fourteen year old high school programming competition to a week long development competition. The hack-a-week was very successful with participation from more than 30 high school students and half a dozen industry sponsors.

In 2014, we're at it again, this time opening to students at all levels aswell as the general public. If you fit into one of these categories, you're in!

  • High School Student
  • University Student
  • Open Category Participant
We strongly encourage collaborations between the categories and there will be special prizes for hybrid teams. Teams can be re-configured on May 2nd to include up to four members.
